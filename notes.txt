Pop (remove last item)                =   O(n)
Push (add new item at the end)        =   O(1)

Shift (remove first item)             =   O(1)
Unshift (adds a new item at the end)  =   O(1)

Insert (add item at an index)         =   O(n)
Delete (at given index)               =   O(n)

Traverse                              =   O(n)
